## 2. Sprint Planning

**Purpose:**  
To define what features and deliverables we’ll complete during Sprint 1 and how we’ll divide responsibilities.

**Scope:**

- Create the **low-fidelity Figma prototype**
- Build the **Product Backlog** in Trello
- Prepare **Block Diagram (Draw.io)**
- Write the **Project Description** and **Scrum Documentation**

**Approach:**  
We reviewed user stories, estimated effort (story points), and prioritized them using the DEEP and INVEST principles.
Tasks were distributed as per member expertise using Trello.

---

## 4. Sprint Retrospective

**Purpose:**  
To reflect on what went well, what didn’t, and what can be improved for the next sprint.

**Outcome:**  
What went well: Team communication and task division.
What could improve: Better time scheduling.
Action for next sprint: Assign deadlines per task and review mid-week.
